What is DukanRit?

DukanRit is a project by Rewardking Thongni for those learning web development and trying to practice to view modify and create their own static simple shopping site that can place order using WhatsApp and hosted on vercel

This site can also be used by small shop owner who want online presence without the needing of expensive software and it's ideal for around 100 order per day

Read the full article to know how to download,modify and host this site for absolutely free

Welcome!

Thank you for checking out this project. I really hope it helps someone out there

I built this template for students, beginners, solo founders, and small business owners who simply want a website without paying for expensive hosting or website builders

Not every business wants to become the next Amazon or Flipkart. Sometimes all you need is a clean website where customers can see what you sell, find your contact information, and discover your business on Google

This project is completely static, built using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's simple by design. While it won't replace platforms like Shopify or WordPress, it's perfect for anyone who just needs an online presence

How to Download and Customize

1. Download or fork this repository from GitHub

2. Upload it to your own GitHub account

3. Open your repository and edit index.html by clicking the ✏️ (Edit) button

You can easily customize everything

-Change your website name by editing the "title" tag

-Change the main heading by editing the header "h1"

-Upload your logo and name it logo.png

-Replace the product images with your own and name them 1.png, 2.png, 3.png, and so on

-Edit the product names, descriptions, prices, hero section, and contact details

4. To get order through WhatsApp find the "YOURNUMBER" and replace it with your own WhatsApp Bussiness number

Commit your changes, and your website will update automatically

Free Hosting

You don't need to pay for hosting.

Just upload your project to GitHub, import it into Vercel, and deploy it

Within a minute, you'll get a free website like:

yourwebsite.vercel.app

If you want people to find your website through Google Search, submit it to Google Search Console and wait for Google to index it. It may take a few days, but once it's indexed, people can find your website through Google.
